Introduction
This guide is written in clear and simple language to help users understand how to use a Smart plug. It is explained in a way that even a first‑time user can understand.  


This guide is intended for: Homeowners, office owners, and first-time users. 


1. What is a Smart Wifi Plug?
A Smart Plug is a small device you plug into your wall socket. Then you plug your appliance (like a fan, lamp, or kettle) into it.
It lets you:
* Turn appliances ON or OFF from your phone 
* Set schedules 
* Save electricity 
* Control it even when you're not at home 



 2. What you will find in the Box


   * Smart Plug Device          
   * Quick Start Paper          
   * Safety Instructions        


Your box may come with only the plug itself, depending on the brand.

Icons:
   * Power Button - Used to turn the Smart Plug on or off

   * Wi-Fi Light – Shows connection status

   * Socket – Where the appliance is connected 


4. Requirements Before You Start 
Before setting up the Smart Plug, make sure you have the following:
      * A working Wi-Fi connection (2.4 GHz). Most smart home devices work this way because 2.4 GHz has a better range. It passes through walls better. It’s more stable for small devices
‘
      * A smartphone (Android or iPhone)

      * The Smart Plug mobile app (brand-specific)

      * A wall socket




5. Setup Guides
Step i. Plug in the Smart Plug 
         * Insert the Smart Plug into your wall socket.

         * The WiFi light should start blinking.
If the light is not blinking, press the power button for 5 seconds to reset.


Step ii. Download the App 
Open your phone and download the app that matches your Smart Plug brand.
For example:
            * TP-Link - Tapo App

            * Amazon - Alexa App

            * Generic Smart Plug - SmartLife App

Step iii. Open the App & Create an Account 
               * Sign in using your email.

               * If you don’t have an account, tap Create Account.

     Tip: Use a password you can remember.

Step iv. Add Your Smart Plug 
Inside the app:
                  * Tap Add Device 

                  * Choose Smart Plug 

                  * Follow the prompts on the screen



Step v. Connect to WiFi 
                     * Select your home WiFi network.

                     * Enter your WiFi password.

                     * Wait for the app to confirm pairing.
 
This may take 10–20 seconds.

8. Troubleshooting Guide
i. Smart Plug not connecting to Wi-Fi
                                       * Make sure your Wi-Fi is 2.4 GHz
                                       * Restart your router or Wi-Fi device. 
                                       * Reset Smart Plug (long-press power button)


ii. The app can’t find the Smart Plug
                                       * Turn phone Bluetooth ON
                                       * Ensure the Smart Plug light is blinking
                                       * Move closer to the router\Wi-Fi


iii. Plug turns on by itself
                                       * Check if you set any schedules
                                       * Remove automation rules




9. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
                                       *  What devices can I use with a Smart Wi-Fi Plug?
 You can use a Smart Wi-Fi Plug with low-power household devices such as lamps, fans, phone chargers, routers, and televisions. Do not use it with high-power appliances like electric heaters, irons, or cookers.


                                          *  Does the Smart Wi-Fi Plug work without internet access?
 The plug needs an internet connection for remote control through the mobile app. However, you can still turn it on or off manually using the power button on the device.


                                             *  What should I do if the Smart Wi-Fi Plug is not connecting to Wi-Fi?
 First, confirm that your Wi-Fi network is active and uses a 2.4 GHz band. Restart your router, then reset the Smart Plug by pressing and holding the power button for 5 seconds before trying again.

                                             *  Is the Smart Wi-Fi Plug safe to use all the time?
 Yes, the Smart Wi-Fi Plug is safe when used as instructed. Always follow safety guidelines, avoid overloading the plug, keep it away from water, and ensure it is used with compatible devices only.
